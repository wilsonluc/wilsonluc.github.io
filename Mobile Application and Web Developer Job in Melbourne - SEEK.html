The Company 

Pixel Technologies design and manufacture world-class electronics solutions for the Lift Industry and are a leading innovator of cutting-edge IoT Solutions.  

Established in 1994, we now have offices in Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane and Singapore, and continue to leave our footprint in the ever-expanding Lift Industry. 

Our head office located in Clifton Hill, Victoria, offers great working conditions with plenty of free on-street parking, is only a short tram ride to the CBD and very close to the eastern freeway entrance. 

Our focus is on quality and reliability as many of our products touch mission-critical infrastructure. 

We combine a fundamental belief in excellence, a passion for our work with a vibrant and collaborative company culture, this is a truly desirable position within the tech industry! 

For more information about Pixel Technologies, please visit www.pixeltechnologies.com.au 

The Role 

We are diversifying our portfolio and updating all our products to include the latest in sensor and IoT technology. As a result, we are on the lookout for a Mobile Application and Web Developer (full-time permanent position) to be part of our diverse Research & Development team. 

This cutting-edge team perform end-to-end design, integration and validation for all our product ranges covering a wide range of key technology areas. With an appreciation of new technologies and future growth markets, allowing us to expand on our product range.  

 Things we prefer you have done or can do. 

  • React and/or React Native experience 

  • iOS and Android application development and deployment 

  • Web applications with a focus on User Experience 

  • GraphQL 

  • Postgres / Elasticsearch 

  • AWS IoT or alternative cloud experience 

  • Understanding and experience with CI/CD, Docker, Git etc. 

  • Excellent written and spoken English skills 

  • Problem solver with strong organisational & time management skills 

  • A willingness to share ideas and collaborate towards the best outcome 

  • An eye for design to create a great customer UI experience 

It’s truly an exciting time to join our professional team and be a part of a cross-disciplinary environment working to deliver robust electronic devices and applications to the world. 

If you are looking for a challenging role with the opportunity to use your existing skills and to expand your skillset further and want to be part of a dynamic and growing company please apply now. 

 

The application form will include these questions:
  • Which of the following statements best describes your right to work in Australia?
  • What's your expected annual base salary?
  • How much notice are you required to give your current employer?
